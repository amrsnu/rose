bio

afters presents a novel perspective on indie rock that blends atmospheric, confessional lyricism with a dark and playful sensuality in soundscapes to birth songs that are deeply evocative and profoundly relatable. the band's marriage of whimsy and gravitas is displayed undeniably in their live performances as well as their recorded tracks, gleefully committing to an emotional and explosive performance each and every time they take the stage.
